ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Women for Women is an Organization based in Wajir County, that advocates for the Economic Empowerment of Women ,Curbing Gender Based Violence in the Society,Providing Therapy for Rape victims, offering pro bono legal services,Offering Mentorship programs under the Big Sister Initiative for girls in Secondary Schools & Sensitizing the youth on HIV/AIDs issues and Civic Education on Counter Violent Extremism.

CORE OBJECTIVES

  1. We are committed to partnering with the local Community to advocate and champion gender equality, health and rights for girls and women in Kenya.

  2. We believe in empowering women and girls by equipping them with the necessary skills in terms of Economic Empowerment and starting income generating projects, Mediation Skills, Education, and counselling through the Big Sister Initiave under the Women for Women Alliance.

  3. We are committed to mentor girls in secondary schools in Kenya by nurturing them in to social conscious leaders and developing the following skills: strong communication skills, time management, entrepreneurship, critical thinking, community involvement, resisting negative peer pressure, self discipline, menstrual hygiene and aspirations for Higher Learning Education.

  4. We believe in teaching Women how to read simple things like signs on the road, numbers on a phone or directions on a medicine bottle which make life a daily struggle.

  5. We are committed to educate the high school girls on menstrual hygiene, taboos associated with menstruation and provide a safe space where girls can interact with us and share and exchange ideas.

  6. Women for Women Alliance stands for Community joining efforts to support the girl child and Women by using the 3P’s that are integral components to our Agenda


  • POWER
    (Self-esteem & Self-efficacy development)

  • PURPOSE
    (Leadership Training and Mentoring)

  • PLATFORM
    (Skills Nurturing)

Let us all join hands in this movement of empowering and advocating for the rights of the women and girls in our Society.
